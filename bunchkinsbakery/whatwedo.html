What We Do

An Experience

Every time you walk into a bakery or restaurant, it’s a feast for all of your senses. Your eyes bounce back the light of a hundred brightly colored treats; your ears are met with hustle and bustle or cozy and calm; your nose is greeted with the warm, sweet, buttery luxury of freshly baked goods. And the second that delectable treat lands on the table, with the sun resting perfectly on the folds of the croissant or the soft cream cheese frosting, as you hold it in your hands and lift it up into your mouth, your taste buds sing a song of celebration.

Themes We've Done

We’ve catered for countless different themed birthday parties, including under-the-sea, construction, princess, charcuterie boards, sewing, art, glitter, color themes, and more. We’ve catered for pride parades/festivals, celebrity events, business grand openings with brand logos incorporated into cakes and cupcakes, countless floral-themed parties and celebrations, and pretty much anything you can dream up, we’re happy to whip up. We provide a wide range of seasonal items, as well as drinks to go with them, including gourmet hot chocolate during winter, watermelon fresca in summer, pumpkin chai during fall, and sweet tea in spring. Our team loves to bake a wide spectrum of treats for people to enjoy- it’s never boring in our kitchen.
