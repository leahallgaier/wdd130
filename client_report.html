Client Report

For my site plan, I decided to use content and images that felt warm and welcoming. I wanted my website to be a place where the viewer wants to pay Bunchkins Bakery a visit.

I communicated with my developer over Microsoft Teams, through the Teams and Chat features. There were some issues with communication at first because there were multiple different conversation threads in different places. The time zone differences also made communication difficult at times. However, there was also success and clarity with the communication as people got used to using Microsoft Teams.

I do not know if my developer met my expectations for my site, because I never received the link to my website from the lead developer, Umaru Kargbo, after I requested it from him.

If I hire a team to do a project for me in the future, I will try to be more readily available for them to clarify issues, confusion, etc.
